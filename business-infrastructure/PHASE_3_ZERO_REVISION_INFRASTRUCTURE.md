# Phase 3: Zero-Revision Infrastructure

This document is the master summary of the Antigravity Phase 3 system.

We have successfully operationalized the 5 reusable website chassis (built in Phase 2) into a sellable, fixed-scope product called the **Crisis Landing Page**.

## The Business Model
- **Product:** Single-page scrollable landing page.
- **Price:** $750 (24h Standard) or $1,050 (12h Urgent).
- **Core Value:** Extreme speed, zero friction.
- **Core Defense:** Zero revisions.

## Why Zero Revisions?
Revision requests destroy profit on fixed-price web builds. This infrastructure prevents revision ambiguity at the door by forcing 100% upfront payment, capturing an exact intake form, and delivering a final product with no back-and-forth communication. 

## The Infrastructure Folders
All operational processes are documented in `/business-infrastructure/`:
- `INTAKE_FORM.md` - The exact fields to capture from the client.
- `GOOGLE_FORM_FIELD_MAP.md` - How to build the intake form.
- `PAYMENT_FLOW.md` - The strict timeline and payment rules.
- `ZERO_REVISION_TERMS.md` - Legal/Checkout copy to protect the scope.
- `TEMPLATE_SELECTION_RULES.md` - How to map the client to a chassis.
- `AI_FILL_RULES.md` - How to handle blank or sloppy client inputs.
- `DELIVERY_WORKFLOW.md` - The steps to close the job.
- `LOOM_SCRIPT.md` - The exact script for the delivery video.
- `CLIENT_DELIVERY_EMAIL.md` - The final email template.

Each of the 5 templates in `/templates/` has been updated to explicitly link back to this infrastructure, ensuring anyone who clones a chassis understands the strict business rules that govern its deployment.
