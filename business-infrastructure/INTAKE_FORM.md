# Crisis Landing Page - Intake Form

This form gathers all the required information to build your landing page in 24 hours.
**Note:** Blank fields will be filled with AI. Sloppy fields will be used as-is. There are no revision rounds.

## Mandatory Fields

1. **Business Name & Tagline**
   - *Example: ApexGrowth - Scaling B2B Brands with AI*
2. **Who is the customer? (One sentence)**
   - *Example: SaaS founders doing over $10k MRR.*
3. **What pain do you solve? (One sentence)**
   - *Example: We stop churn before it happens so you keep your recurring revenue.*
4. **What is the ONE action visitors should take?**
   - *Options: Buy, Book, Signup, Call, or Submit form.*
5. **Link to Logo**
   - *Please provide a URL to a high-res logo (Dropbox, Drive, etc.).*
6. **Brand Colors / Hex Codes**
   - *Example: #0F172A (Primary), #3B82F6 (Accent)*
7. **Testimonial or Social Proof Text**
   - *Example: "They increased our conversions by 40% in a week." - John D.*
8. **Domain Choice**
   - *Options: Vercel subdomain, Netlify subdomain, or Client-owned domain.*
9. **Offer Name**
   - *Example: The 90-Day Scaling Program*
10. **Offer Price (If applicable)**
    - *Example: $1,500*
11. **Contact Email**
    - *Email address to display on the page.*
12. **CTA Button Text**
    - *Example: "Book Your Free Audit"*
13. **CTA Destination Link**
    - *Example: Calendly link, Stripe checkout link, etc.*
14. **Any Images/Assets Link**
    - *Link to a folder with any product or team photos.*
15. **Competitor/Example Website Link (Optional)**
    - *Link to a site you like the style of.*
16. **Deadline Type**
    - *Options: 24h Standard or 12h Urgent*
