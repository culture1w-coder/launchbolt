# Template Selection Rules

Use this guide to map the client's business type to the correct chassis. Do not overthink it. The goal is speed.

## The Mapping Guide

1. **SaaS / Tool**
   - Software, SaaS, micro-SaaS
   - Apps (Web or Mobile)
   - AI tools
   - Automation products

2. **Creator / Course**
   - Coaches and Consultants selling knowledge
   - Course creators
   - Personal brands
   - Paid communities

3. **Local Service**
   - Contractors (Roofing, HVAC, Plumbing)
   - Local businesses (Gyms, Salons, Dentists)
   - Repair services
   - Home service providers

4. **Product / Drop**
   - Physical products
   - Ecommerce brands
   - Limited merchandise drops
   - One-product funnel offers

5. **Agency / Consultant**
   - Marketing Agencies
   - AI Automation Agencies (AIAA)
   - B2B Service Providers
   - High-ticket consultants offering "done for you" services

## Fallback Rule
If you are unsure which chassis to use:
- Use **Agency / Consultant** for any B2B or digital service.
- Use **Local Service** for any local, offline, or brick-and-mortar business.
