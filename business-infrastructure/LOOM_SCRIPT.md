# Loom Video Script

Keep the video between 2-3 minutes. Speak clearly and professionally. Do not ask for feedback.

## Script

"Hey [Client Name], your page is done.

Here’s your page. *[Scroll through the live URL]* As you can see, we mapped all the copy, colors, and branding directly from your intake form into the design.

Here’s the live URL so you can view it immediately: *[Point to browser address bar]*

Here’s the GitHub repo: *[Switch tab to GitHub]* This contains all the code for your site. You fully own this.

If you chose to use your own domain, here’s how to connect or update your DNS: *[Briefly explain pointing A records to Vercel/Netlify, or reference the email instructions]*

This was built from your intake according to the fixed 24h crisis build scope. 

As a reminder, no revision rounds are included under this package. The site is live and ready to accept traffic. 

Thanks for trusting us with the build, and best of luck with the launch!"
