# Google Form Field Map

Map these fields to your Google Form or Typeform. Make all fields **Required** except the competitor link and asset link.

- **Field 1:** Short Answer | Business Name & Tagline
- **Field 2:** Short Answer | Who is your exact target customer? (One sentence max)
- **Field 3:** Short Answer | What specific pain do you solve for them? (One sentence max)
- **Field 4:** Multiple Choice | What is the ONE action you want visitors to take? (Buy, Book, Signup, Call, Submit form)
- **Field 5:** Short Answer | Link to your logo (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
- **Field 6:** Short Answer | Brand colors (Hex codes if you have them)
- **Field 7:** Paragraph | Provide one strong testimonial or piece of social proof text
- **Field 8:** Multiple Choice | How will this be hosted? (Free Subdomain, Client-owned domain)
- **Field 9:** Short Answer | What is the exact name of your offer?
- **Field 10:** Short Answer | Offer price (Enter N/A if not applicable)
- **Field 11:** Short Answer | Contact email address to display on the site
- **Field 12:** Short Answer | What should the main button say? (e.g. "Get Started")
- **Field 13:** Short Answer | Where should the button link to? (Calendly, Checkout, etc.)
- **Field 14:** Short Answer | Link to any images/assets we should use (Optional)
- **Field 15:** Short Answer | Link to a competitor or website you like (Optional)
- **Field 16:** Multiple Choice | Timeline requested (24h Standard, 12h Urgent [+$300])

**Description Text for the Form:**
*Welcome. Please fill out this form completely. We will build your page exactly to these specifications within the agreed timeline. There are no revision rounds, so please double-check your spelling and links.*
