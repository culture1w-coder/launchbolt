# Payment Flow Process

This document outlines the strict payment and operational process.

## The Flow

1. **Client Selection:** Client selects package (Crisis Page $750 or Crisis Page + Form $1,000).
2. **Payment:** Client pays 100% upfront through a Stripe payment link or PayPal Business invoice.
3. **Intake:** After successful payment, the client is redirected to complete the Intake Form.
4. **Timer Start:** The 24h (or 12h) timer starts **only** after payment clears AND the intake form is fully submitted.
5. **Template Selection:** Builder clones the closest matching chassis based on the client's business type.
6. **Execution:** Builder swaps copy and assets using the intake form data.
7. **Deployment:** Builder deploys the site to Vercel/Netlify.
8. **Recording:** Builder records a 2-3 minute Loom video walking through the final product.
9. **Delivery:** Builder sends one final delivery email with the live URL, GitHub repo, and Loom video.
10. **Closeout:** Job is complete.

## Hard Operating Rules

- No back-and-forth communication.
- No clarification calls.
- No unpaid copy changes.
- No revision rounds.
- 100% payment upfront.
- Do not start the delivery timer until payment clears.
- No deposits.
- No milestones.
- No exceptions.
